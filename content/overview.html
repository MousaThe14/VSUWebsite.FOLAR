FOLAR

Friends of Lower Appomattox River, or FOLAR, is a nonprofit organization actively working to preserve, protect, and enhance the Lower Appomattox River for all to enjoy.

Collective Impact:
Developing a regional common agenda around creating and protecting the Appomattox River and Trail to positively impact public health, safety, and community prosperity.

Appomattox River Trail

The Appomattox River Trail is a 25 mile long trail along the lower Appomattox River with access points connecting to boat access, parks, and other riverside trails. It encompasses the counties of Chesterfield, Dinwiddie and Prince George, and the cities of Colonial Heights, Hopewell and Petersburg.
